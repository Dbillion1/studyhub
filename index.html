❓ FAQ

Common questions

Is StudyHub free to use? +
Yes! StudyHub has a generous free tier that includes AI tutor access, quizzes, flashcards, revision notes, Prompt Lab, planner and community access. You upgrade for better workflows: advanced planning, analytics, reports, exam coaching and premium support.
Which subjects does StudyHub cover? +
Currently we cover Mathematics, English (Language & Literature), Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics), Geography, History, Computer Science, French, and German. We're expanding to more subjects every month.
How is the AI tutor different from ChatGPT? +
StudyHub's tutor is specifically trained and configured for secondary school students. It knows your subjects, your year group, your target grades, and your weak areas. It adapts its explanations to your level, creates personalised quizzes, and proactively recommends what to study next.
Is my data safe? +
Absolutely. We take student privacy very seriously. We comply with GDPR, never sell your data, and use enterprise-grade encryption. Your revision data and conversations are yours and never shared.
Can my parents see my progress? +
On the Ultimate plan, we offer optional parent progress reports that show revision time, quiz scores, and subject performance. This feature requires the student's consent to activate.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. Cancel anytime with no questions asked. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period, and your revision data is always yours to keep.
